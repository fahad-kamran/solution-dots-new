Accurate Mobile ID Scanning with No Compromise on Speed

Our ID scanning software securely and quickly extracts data from 2000+ identity documents worldwide.

  • Scan ID cards, visa stickers, passports, drivers' licenses and military IDs in less than 1 second.
  • Scan Visual Inspection Zones (VIZ), Machine Readable Zones (MRZ) and PDF417 barcodes.
  • 100% scan accuracy rate for IDs with PDF417, industry-leading accuracy for other documents.

Fake ID Detection Software

Reduce human error, prevent fraud and aid regulatory compliance with instant, automated ID validation.

  • Run authenticity and validity checks to identify fake identity documents in 1 second.
  • Designed for use where time is of the essence, such as age- verified delivery, delivery driver onboarding and car rental.
  • No need for an internet connection, fraud checks can happen on the device.

Protecting Data is Paramount

Security is vital when your business is processing identity data. Our processing and transmission methods are designed to protect personal data.

  • Compliant with applicable data privacy laws and business regulations, including GDPR and CCPA.
  • All data processing happens on device, unless verification method requires internet connection.
  • Any transmitted data is encrypted and anonymized and no personal data is stored on the device.

With the post pandemic migration to cloud enabling businesses to do more with their data, both the opportunities and challenges associated with Al have grown more complex. For many organizations (banks, for example) Al is not part of their core competencies-and that's where Al services can help. Artificial Intelligence consulting services help businesses identify where Al could make them more efficient, more profitable, or open up new revenue streams, and then help them implement Al through strategies, operating models and change management programs. Al services also support the integration of Al solutions into core business functions and processes. Specific examples include: Al for marketing, helping companies get more out of their marketing spend using data and Al; or Al for processing, helping companies process information or data more efficiently; or Al for customer engagement, improving customer service for companies with tools like Al chatbots. Al services are applicable across a wide range of industries.
